Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-11-23

Data Collection

LifeMap stores your documents in your personal Google Drive account. We do not access, store, or process your documents on our servers. All document data remains under your control in your own Google Drive storage.

Google Drive Access

We request access to your Google Drive only to:

  • Create and manage files in the "LifeMap Documents" folder
  • Store and retrieve your document metadata
  • Organize your personal document graph structure

We use the following minimal Google Drive API scopes:

  • drive.file - Access only files created by LifeMap
  • drive.appdata - Store application configuration data
  • drive.metadata - Read file metadata for document organization

OpenAI Processing

When you use the AI document analysis feature, document content is sent to OpenAI's API for processing. This enables intelligent document categorization and relationship detection. Documents are processed by OpenAI but are not stored permanently by them. Please review OpenAI's Privacy Policy for more information about their data handling practices.

Data Ownership

You own all your data. Your documents, metadata, and graph structure are stored in your Google Drive account and remain under your complete control. You can:

  • Access all your LifeMap documents directly in Google Drive
  • Delete your LifeMap documents at any time
  • Revoke LifeMap's access to your Google Drive through your Google Account settings
  • Export your data from Google Drive using standard Google Drive tools

Data Security

LifeMap is a client-side application that runs entirely in your browser. We implement the following security measures:

  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • OAuth 2.0 authentication with Google
  • No server-side storage of your personal data
  • Secure token management for API access

Local Storage

LifeMap uses browser local storage to cache your graph data for improved performance. This data is stored only on your device and can be cleared through your browser settings at any time.

Analytics and Monitoring

We may collect anonymous usage analytics to improve the application, including:

  • Page views and navigation patterns
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Performance metrics
  • Error reports (without personal information)

These analytics do not include any of your document content or personal information.

Third-Party Services

LifeMap integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google Drive API - For document storage and retrieval
  • Google OAuth - For secure authentication
  • OpenAI API - For optional AI document analysis

Each service has its own privacy policy that governs their data handling practices.

Children's Privacy

LifeMap is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in Google Drive
  • Correct or update your information
  • Delete your LifeMap data
  • Revoke access permissions
  • Export your data

To exercise these rights, you can manage your data directly through Google Drive or revoke LifeMap's access through your Google Account settings.
